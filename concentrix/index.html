About the Game

Concentrix is a music-driven puzzle game where every move is in sync with the beat. Spin concentric rings to match color patterns before the track ends or chase the highest score across every loop.

The challenge is based on whatever music you choose, making every session feel different. Free to play with no ads, and 5 full tracks across all modes and difficulties. Unlock 35+ tracks and import your own music library with a one-time premium purchase.

Two ways to play

  • Solve: Clear the board as many times as you can before the track finishes
  • Score: Maximize your points across multiple loops

Features

  • Three difficulty levels with separate leaderboards
  • Free to play with no ads: 5 full tracks across all modes and difficulties
  • Premium: 35+ additional tracks, plus import your own music library and set the BPM (via Tunebat-style lookup or the in-game tap-to-measure feature)
  • Vibrant, randomly generated color schemes every game...or set your own custom color theme
  • Beat-synced animations and haptic feedback
  • High score tracking across every track, mode, and difficulty
  • Localized in 9 languages

What Makes It Different

The music is the puzzle.

The challenge is generated from whatever track is playing, so difficulty and pacing change with the song. No two sessions play the same.

Bring your own soundtrack.

With premium, play to any song in your music library: import tracks and set the BPM via lookup or the in-game tap-to-measure feature.

Everything is on the beat.

Moves, animations, and haptic feedback are all synced to the track, so playing feels less like solving and more like keeping rhythm.

About butterssoft

Formed as a solo indie game developer in Den Haag, Netherlands, butterssoft makes games with unique mechanics or takes an established genre and puts an interesting twist on it. Concentrix is the first release with a few more puzzle based games soon to come, and after that some parody games that aim to make players laugh.
